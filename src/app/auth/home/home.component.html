Why another websites?

As a high school junior I was searching for options to do internships in companies. I checked with my school counselor but I was told they don't have any contacts with companies. Then I started googling around, to my surprise I didn’t find any dedicated websites for high school students. So decided to create one and this website is my first step towards making highschool internships more accessible.
